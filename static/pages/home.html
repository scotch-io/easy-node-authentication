Electroneum wallets for mining

Don't mine directly to Cryptopia! This pool doesn't support wallet addresses with Payment ID. Please consider the following options instead.
Official Electroneum mobile wallet
You'll be able to spend or receive Electroneum coins with your mobile wallet (in the app) and use your mobile wallet address in Windows, Mac and Linux CPU mining software that will run from your desktop PC and mine more Electroneum with help from our pool.
Official paper wallet
An offline wallet is a public Electroneum wallet that you can transfer funds to that was not generated on the Internet. Please keep the generated PDF document safe and secure. There is no way to recover it if it's lost or stolen.
